## About Me
### Fullstack Developer (BaaS/CaaS) & Tech Consultant
I provide end-to-end engineering through **Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS)** and **Credit-as-a-Service (CaaS)** models, specializing in **Vue.js** for reactive frontends and **.NET APIs** for robust financial backends. My primary focus is building scalable, event-driven infrastructure. I also carry strategic C-level consulting to align technical architecture with business growth.

### 🔭 Current Focus
* **Advising:** Financial tech strategy and infrastructure for digital marketing, health, and aesthetics business units.
* **Restructuring:** Overhauling engineering workflows to support high-compliance financial products and credit systems.

### 🛠️ Technical Specialization
* **Fintech Backend:** Architecting secure **.NET APIs** and serverless workflows using **AWS Lambda**.
* **Event-Driven Systems:** Implementing reliable asynchronous messaging and queuing via **AWS SQS and SNS**.
* **Frontend:** **Vue.js Specialist** building complex, data-heavy dashboards and reactive financial interfaces.
* **Infrastructure:** Leveraging **BaaS/CaaS** layers to enable embedded banking and credit capabilities.

### 🧠 Core Philosophy
* **Financial Systems Thinking:** Bridging the gap between executive vision and the complex reality of modern fintech execution.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Automating credit workflows and banking operations via cloud-native automation.
* **Strategy & Execution:** I don't just consult on the fintech roadmap; I architect and build the ledger.
